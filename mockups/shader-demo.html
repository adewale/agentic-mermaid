≈ Agentic Mermaid the living mark

A little tumult of delight

The logo is a layered directed graph, after Kozo Sugiyama's method for drawing DAGs. The flow converges downward: three source nodes route through dummy nodes in the middle rank to one sink, so the layered drawing vaguely resembles a trident, emergent from the layout rather than drawn as one. A WebGL shader sweeps a soft light down through the ranks now and then. It settles to one still frame under reduced-motion and quickens on hover.

​

Shown large so the motion reads; in the nav it is the size of the mark on the left.
